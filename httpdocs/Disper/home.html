Our approach

Secure your finances

Wider distribution of your stock means more attention and less aggressive fluctuation. Small and mid-cap companies are, for the most part, not well known amongst foreign investors. By creating a brighter spotlight, you will increase access to the capital market in the near and far future. Higher activity will attract more and bigger investors, that will never change. Disper offers you access to capital and exposure.

Market creating

Reach the new generation of investors, with a higher risk profile, through open, visual and concise communication on their preferred media channels. We will assist you building a much larger crowd for your exchange listed company. We will make sure that your mission statement, product and stock, will be seen and understood by a larger crowd. Clear communication is the way to attract investors. And always remember, “you create the news, the media follows the news”.

Increase your number of shareholders

When it comes to small and mid-cap companies, professional investors usually operate internationally, private more nationally. We utilise the best foreign, EU, trading platforms to make your stock and information accessible to the right audience. As a listed company, you’re selling your product to at least two different audiences.
